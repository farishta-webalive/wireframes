Who We Are

Steel Stairs & Balustrade Systems, Melbourne

We are an innovative steel fabrication company based in Melbourne, Australia. Our speciality is in the design, manufacture and installation of steel stairs and balustrade systems for the domestic and commercial market. We produce budget factory stairs through to the highest quality architectural stairs. Our work is mainly throughout Melbourne, but we have done and are capable of interstate work.

The majority of our balustrades are stainless steel, and we have experience in glass, either co-ordinating with glaziers or installing glass ourselves. We are known through the steel fabrication industry for the high quality of our workmanship, but we vary this according to the environment that our work is going into, and the budget that is available.

We build our staircases to the Building Code of Australia.

This web site is set up to market our company and to educate potential clients about stairs, minimizing the confusion when discussing their projects with us. Try to look at the other pages within the web site to familiarize yourself with the terms and criteria that apply to stairs then you can call, fax or email us.

What We Build

Our Specialisations

From elegant central spine staircases to precision flat bar stringers — every system is designed, fabricated and installed in-house.

Central Spine Stairs

A single central stringer supports each tread, creating an open, floating aesthetic. Available in a wide range of finishes for maximum architectural impact.

Flat Bar Stringers

Twin flat bar stringers running along the sides — a clean, modern look suited to both residential and commercial applications across Melbourne.

Balustrade Systems

Stainless steel balustrades and glass panels, either co-ordinated with specialist glaziers or installed by our team for a seamless finish.
